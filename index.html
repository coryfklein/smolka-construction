Why Smolka Construction?

Smolka Construction has operated throughout northern Utah for over 20 years. We are very familiar with the area and have many connections with local businesses as well as an intimate knowledge of building homes in Utah. We have a reputation for getting things done right the first time, and for creating a no-hassle, stress-free experience for our customers. The wide selection and variety of previous experience demonstrates our expertise, as seen in our portfolio.

Our business is unique in the Salt Lake area. We are family owned, with Clarey Smolka focusing on the bidding and financial aspects of the projects, while Joe Smolka operates on site and oversees all construction, and also provides expertise in all aspects of the project. This accounts for our many return customers, and the fact that many of our sub-contractors choose us when they need work done, including two architects' offices and an architect's home, among others. Our subs are trustworthy, and we make sure they provide the services promised.
